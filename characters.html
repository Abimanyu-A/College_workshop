Ghibli Characters

Chihiro Ogino

The protagonist of Spirited Away, Chihiro finds herself trapped in a mysterious world and must work to free herself and her parents.

San

A fierce warrior raised by wolves in Princess Mononoke, San fights to protect the forest from human destruction.

Howl Pendragon

A powerful and eccentric wizard from Howl’s Moving Castle, known for his vanity and his magical castle.

Totoro

A friendly forest spirit from My Neighbor Totoro, Totoro is a symbol of nature’s magic and wonder.

Kiki

A young witch from Kiki's Delivery Service, Kiki embarks on a journey of self-discovery while running her own flying delivery service.

Nausicaä

The brave princess of the Valley of the Wind, Nausicaä seeks harmony between humans and nature while navigating a post-apocalyptic world.
