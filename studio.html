Discover the magic behind the legendary animation studio that has inspired generations worldwide.

Our Legacy

Founded in 1985 by visionary filmmakers Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, Studio Ghibli has crafted timeless classics such as "Spirited Away," "My Neighbor Totoro," and "Princess Mononoke." Known for breathtaking animation, deep storytelling, and emotional depth, the studio remains a symbol of artistic excellence.
